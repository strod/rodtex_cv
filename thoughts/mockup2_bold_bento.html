Hello! I'm Rodrigo.

I'm a Marketing Analytics Director with 8+ years of experience building end-to-end MarTech solutions, data infrastructure, and measurement systems. Based in Sao Paulo, Brazil, I combine deep technical skill in Python, SQL, GCP, and AWS with strategic marketing analytics expertise.

My expertise spans the full data stack — from data warehouse architecture (Redshift, BigQuery) to ML models (Marketing Mix Modeling, Attribution, RFM segmentation) to executive dashboards. I build ETL pipelines, API integrations, and automated reporting systems that transform raw marketing data into actionable insights.

With an MBA in Data Science & Analytics from USP, an MSc in Industrial Engineering from COPPE-UFRJ, and dual Bachelor's degrees in Mechanical Engineering and Physics from UFF, I combine academic rigor with hands-on engineering expertise. My career spans major Brazilian agencies, AI startups, and international e-commerce.
