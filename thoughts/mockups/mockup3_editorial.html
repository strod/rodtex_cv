00 — About

A few words
about myself

With 8+ years of experience at the intersection of marketing and technology, I have built a career around turning raw data into strategic insight. From writing Python pipelines that process millions of ad impressions to architecting cloud-based analytics platforms on GCP and AWS, my work sits precisely where engineering discipline meets marketing intuition.

My journey has taken me through some of Brazil's leading agencies and media companies, including Rede Globo, WMcCann (McCann Worldgroup), and IPG Mediabrands, as well as AI startups (MINT) and international e-commerce (Filterbuy). Along the way I earned an MBA in Data Science from USP, pursued an MSc in Industrial Engineering at COPPE-UFRJ, and hold dual Bachelor's degrees in Mechanical Engineering and Physics from UFF.

I believe the best analytics work is invisible: clean data, clear dashboards, and decisions that feel obvious in hindsight. When I'm not writing code or digging into campaign data, you'll find me exploring new JavaScript frameworks, contributing to open-source projects, or thinking about the next thing to automate.
